Mission: To provide the full spectrum of HR and IR services to clients in a manner that is fair to both the employer and the employee.

Vision: To become the premier HR consultancy in the country, known for making a difference in how organizations are run and positively impacting people's daily lives.

Core Values: Communication, Accountability, Transparency, Honesty, Respect, Integrity, Flexibility, and Excellence.


**360HR** (based in Johannesburg) positions itself as a premium, comprehensive "full circle" HR partner. Their primary angle is offering flexible, outsourced HR solutions so business owners can focus on their core operations without worrying about compliance, CCMA risks, or payroll headaches.

If you are building an HR consultancy website and want to model it after 360HR's structure, here is a blueprint for the layout and copy strategy.

### **1. The Strategy & Brand Voice**

* **The Hook:** "You focus on growing your business; we’ll handle your people."
* **Brand Voice:** Professional, reassuring, authoritative, and structured.
* **Target Audience:** SMEs with no dedicated HR department, and larger corporations needing specialized support for existing HR teams.
* **The "Fear/Relief" Angle:** They gently remind businesses of the risks of non-compliance (heavy CCMA fines, Department of Labour penalties) and offer themselves as the protective shield.

---

### **2. Website Structure & Wireframe**

Here is how you can model your website’s pages and flow based on their successful formula:

#### **A. The Homepage (The "Full Circle" Overview)**

* **Hero Section:**
* *Headline Idea:* Complete HR Solutions for South African Businesses.
* *Sub-headline:* From compliance and payroll to dispute resolution. We manage your workforce so you can manage your business.
* *Call to Action (CTA):* Get a Free Consultation / View Our Services.


* **Value Proposition (Why an HR Specialist is Important):**
* Briefly highlight the risks of non-compliance (CCMA awards, Dept of Labour fines) and how your services mitigate them.


* **Service Pillars (Grid Layout):**
* Use icons and short descriptions for your core offerings (e.g., *Labour Relations, Outsourced HR, Skills Development, Recruitment*).


* **Social Proof:**
* "Over X Years Experience," "X+ Businesses Serviced." Add a client testimonial section here.



#### **B. The "Services" Page (Categorized for clarity)**

360HR groups their massive list of services into neat categories. You should structure your services page similarly:

* **HR Outsourcing & Administration:** Payroll management, employment contracts, HR policies, employee handbooks, and statutory compliance.
* **Industrial & Labour Relations (IR):** Disciplinary hearings, CCMA referrals, retrenchments, strike management, and union negotiations.
* **Skills Development & Employment Equity:** Workplace Skills Plans (WSP), Annual Training Reports (ATR), SETA accreditation, and learnerships.
* **B-BBEE Consulting:** Scorecard verification, gap analysis, and strategy.
* **Talent Acquisition & Recruitment:** Sourcing, background checks, and onboarding.
* **Wellness & Change Management:** Employee Assistance Programmes (EAPs), mental health awareness, and restructuring.

#### **C. The "About Us" Page**

* **The Philosophy:** State your mission. (e.g., 360HR uses *"Empower | Inspire | Believe"*).
* **The Leadership:** Feature the founder(s) or director(s). Highlight qualifications (e.g., SABPP registration, years of corporate experience, legal background). People buy into HR based on trust and credentials.
* **Who We Serve:** Clarify that you offer both *ad-hoc support* for once-off projects and *monthly retainers* for ongoing management.

#### **D. The "Contact" Page**

* Keep it simple: Phone, Email, Office Location, and a clean contact form. Mention if consultations are "by appointment only."

### **3. Copywriting Templates (Adapted for Your Site)**

If you are writing the copy for this site, here is how you can emulate 360HR's style without plagiarizing:

**For the Homepage Intro:**

> *"Every business is fueled by its people, but managing human resources requires time, specialized knowledge, and constant attention to changing labour laws. We provide affordable, flexible HR outsourcing solutions designed to support your organization. Whether you need a complete outsourced HR department or specialist support for your in-house team, we provide reliable advice to keep you compliant and productive."*

**For the Labour Relations Section:**

> *"Navigating South African labour law can be complex and risky. An unfavourable CCMA award or a Department of Labour fine can devastate a business. We act as your shield, ensuring absolute compliance. Our IR experts handle disciplinary hearings, grievances, retrenchments, and CCMA representation professionally and fairly."*

**For the Payroll / Admin Section:**

> *"HR does far more than just issue payslips, but getting the administration right is the foundation of a happy workforce. We remove the administrative burden by managing your payroll, updating employment contracts, drafting company policies, and ensuring all statutory deductions (UIF, PAYE, SDL) are flawlessly executed."*
